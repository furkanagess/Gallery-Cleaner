# Swipe Feature Visual Prompt - Nano Banana
## Swipe Left Delete, Swipe Right Keep

Create an ultra-modern, clear visual showcasing the swipe-to-delete and swipe-to-keep feature for Gallery Cleaner app. The design should clearly demonstrate the Tinder-style swipe gesture with intuitive left/right actions.

**Format & Dimensions:**
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 portrait (1080x1920px) for mobile screens
- Alternative: 16:9 landscape (2048x1152px) for banners
- Alternative: 1:1 square (1080x1080px) for social media
- High resolution, crisp details, professional quality
- Optimized for mobile app screenshots and marketing

**Main Composition:**
A clear, modern visual demonstrating the swipe gesture interface. The design should show a photo card being swiped with clear left (delete) and right (keep) indicators, motion trails, and intuitive visual feedback.

**Visual Elements:**

1. **Central Photo Card:**
   - Main photo card in center of composition
   - Realistic photo thumbnail (can be generic landscape, portrait, or abstract)
   - Card with rounded corners (20-24px radius)
   - Soft shadow beneath card for depth
   - Slightly tilted/rotated showing swipe motion
   - Modern, clean card design with subtle border

2. **Swipe Left - Delete Action:**
   - **Left Side Indicators:**
     * Large red "X" icon on left side of screen
     * Red delete icon (trash can or X symbol)
     * Text: "DELETE" or "SWIPE LEFT TO DELETE" in red
     * Red motion trail/arrow pointing left
     * Red glow or highlight effect
     * Warning/alert visual style
   
   - **Visual Effects:**
     * Photo card tilted left (showing left swipe gesture)
     * Motion blur effect trailing to the left
     * Red color overlay on left side of card
     * Red particles or effects suggesting deletion
     * Arrow or gesture line showing left direction
     * Card fading/transparent on left edge

3. **Swipe Right - Keep Action:**
   - **Right Side Indicators:**
     * Large green checkmark icon on right side of screen
     * Green keep icon (checkmark or heart symbol)
     * Text: "KEEP" or "SWIPE RIGHT TO KEEP" in green
     * Green motion trail/arrow pointing right
     * Green glow or highlight effect
     * Success/positive visual style
   
   - **Visual Effects:**
     * Photo card tilted right (showing right swipe gesture)
     * Motion blur effect trailing to the right
     * Green color overlay on right side of card
     * Green particles or effects suggesting keeping
     * Arrow or gesture line showing right direction
     * Card bright/clear on right edge

4. **Dual Action Visualization (Recommended):**
   - Split screen or side-by-side comparison
   - Left side: Swipe left delete action
   - Right side: Swipe right keep action
   - OR: Single card with both indicators visible
   - Clear visual separation between actions

5. **Gesture Indicators:**
   - **Finger/Hand Gesture:**
     * Subtle finger or hand icon showing swipe motion
     * Can be stylized, minimalist hand gesture
     * Motion lines showing swipe direction
     * Touch point indicator on card
   
   - **Motion Trails:**
     * Smooth, curved motion lines
     * Directional arrows
     * Velocity indicators (speed lines)
     * Swipe path visualization

6. **Photo Deck/Stack:**
   - Stack of photo cards behind main card
   - Showing multiple photos in deck
   - Depth and layering effect
   - Cards getting smaller as they go back
   - Subtle shadows between cards
   - Modern card stack design

7. **Color Palette (App Colors):**
   - **Primary Blue**: #5D9CEC (for UI elements, background accents)
   - **Delete Red**: #EF4444 (for left swipe, delete action)
   - **Keep Green**: #22C55E (for right swipe, keep action)
   - **Background**: 
     * Light: #F8FAFC or #FFFFFF
     * Dark: #191C24 (dark theme option)
   - **Card Background**: White (#FFFFFF) or dark card (#1E293B)
   - **Text**: 
     * Dark: #1E293B (light theme)
     * Light: #E2E8F0 (dark theme)
   - **Accents**: Subtle gradients and glows

8. **Typography:**
   - **Main Text**: "Swipe Left to Delete" / "Swipe Right to Keep"
   - **Action Labels**: "DELETE" (red), "KEEP" (green)
   - **Instructions**: Clear, readable, modern font
   - **Font Style**: Bold, sans-serif (Poppins-style)
   - **Size**: Large enough to be readable on mobile
   - **Contrast**: High contrast for readability

9. **Modern UI Elements:**
   - Clean, minimalist design
   - Rounded corners throughout
   - Soft shadows and depth
   - Glassmorphism effects (optional)
   - Smooth gradients
   - Modern app interface aesthetic
   - iOS/Android design language

10. **Visual Hierarchy:**
    - Main photo card as focal point
    - Clear left/right action zones
    - Prominent action indicators
    - Supporting elements don't compete
    - Balanced composition
    - Eye flow from center to actions
