# Gyroscope Swipe Feature - Video Production Prompt

## Video Concept
Create a short, engaging demonstration video showcasing the innovative gyroscope-based swipe feature in Gallery Cleaner app, where users can control photo swiping by physically tilting their phone left or right.

## Video Structure

### Scene 1: Introduction (0-3 seconds)
- **Visual**: Close-up of a smartphone screen showing the Gallery Cleaner app's swipe interface
- **Action**: A photo card is visible in the center with "Keep" (green) and "Delete" (red) indicators on the sides
- **Text Overlay**: "Swipe with Motion" or "Tilt to Swipe"
- **Style**: Modern, clean UI with smooth animations

### Scene 2: Traditional Swipe Demo (3-6 seconds)
- **Visual**: Hand gesture showing traditional finger swipe (left/right)
- **Action**: User swipes a photo left (delete) and right (keep) using finger gestures
- **Text Overlay**: "Traditional Swipe" or "Finger Swipe"
- **Purpose**: Establish baseline understanding before introducing new feature

### Scene 3: Gyroscope Feature Introduction (6-10 seconds)
- **Visual**: Phone held in landscape orientation, user's hands visible
- **Action**: 
  - Phone tilts slightly to the right → Photo swipes right (Keep animation)
  - Phone tilts slightly to the left → Photo swipes left (Delete animation)
- **Text Overlay**: "Tilt Your Phone" or "Motion Control"
- **Visual Effects**: 
  - Subtle phone rotation animation
  - Photo card smoothly follows phone movement
  - Haptic feedback indicator (subtle vibration icon)

### Scene 4: Detailed Motion Demonstration (10-18 seconds)
- **Visual**: Split screen or side-by-side view
  - Left side: Phone being tilted
  - Right side: App screen showing photo swiping
- **Actions**:
  1. **Right Tilt (Keep)**: 
     - Phone tilts right → Green "Keep" badge appears
     - Photo animates off-screen to the right
     - Success sound effect
  2. **Left Tilt (Delete)**:
     - Phone tilts left → Red "Delete" badge appears
     - Photo animates off-screen to the left
     - Delete sound effect
  3. **Rapid Succession**: Show multiple photos being processed quickly
- **Text Overlay**: 
  - "Tilt Right = Keep"
  - "Tilt Left = Delete"
  - "Fast & Intuitive"

### Scene 5: Use Case Scenarios (18-25 seconds)
- **Scenario 1**: User reviewing photos while walking
  - Phone in hand, natural tilting motion
  - Quick decision-making flow
- **Scenario 2**: One-handed operation
  - User holds phone with one hand
  - Tilts phone to make decisions
  - Emphasizes convenience
- **Visual**: Real-world usage context, natural lighting

### Scene 6: Technical Highlight (25-30 seconds)
- **Visual**: 
  - Smooth, responsive animations
  - No lag or delay between motion and action
  - Cooldown period indicator (subtle visual feedback)
- **Text Overlay**: 
  - "Smooth Motion Detection"
  - "800ms Cooldown Protection"
  - "Prevents Accidental Swipes"

### Scene 7: Feature Benefits (30-35 seconds)
- **Visual**: Quick montage showing:
  - Faster photo review process
  - One-handed convenience
  - Intuitive natural motion
  - Accessibility benefits
- **Text Overlay**: 
  - "Faster Review"
  - "One-Handed Use"
  - "Natural Motion"
  - "Accessible Design"

### Scene 8: Call to Action (35-40 seconds)
- **Visual**: App icon with download/store badges
- **Text Overlay**: 
  - "Try Gallery Cleaner Now"
  - App Store / Play Store badges
  - "Download Free"
- **Background**: App screenshots or feature highlights

## Technical Specifications

### Video Format
- **Duration**: 30-40 seconds (optimal for social media)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical/portrait) for mobile-first platforms
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 (Full HD vertical)
- **Frame Rate**: 60fps for smooth motion capture
- **Audio**: 
  - Upbeat, modern background music
  - Subtle sound effects for swipe actions
  - No voiceover (text-based communication)

### Visual Style
- **Color Scheme**: 
  - Primary: App's blue (#5D9CEC)
  - Success: Green (#22C55E) for Keep
  - Error: Red (#EF4444) for Delete
  - Background: Dark theme (#191C24) or Light theme (#F8FAFC)
- **Typography**: 
  - Modern, bold sans-serif
  - High contrast for readability
  - Animated text transitions
- **Animation Style**:
  - Smooth, fluid motion
  - Ease-in-out curves
  - Subtle bounce effects
  - Professional polish

### Camera Work
- **Phone Recording**: 
  - Use actual device with screen recording
  - Show real-time gyroscope response
  - Natural hand movements
- **Screen Recording**: 
  - High-quality screen capture
  - Smooth 60fps recording
  - No UI lag or stuttering
- **Hand/Phone Shots**:
  - Clean, well-lit environment
  - Focus on phone screen and hand position
  - Show natural tilting motion

### Motion Detection Visualization
- **Optional Enhancement**: 
  - Overlay accelerometer data visualization
  - Show motion threshold indicators
  - Display tilt angle (subtle, non-intrusive)
  - Visual feedback for motion detection

## Key Messages to Convey

1. **Innovation**: "First-of-its-kind motion-based photo management"
2. **Convenience**: "One-handed, intuitive control"
3. **Speed**: "Review photos faster with natural motion"
4. **Accessibility**: "Easy to use for everyone"
5. **Modern**: "Cutting-edge technology meets user experience"

## Production Notes

### Pre-Production
- Test gyroscope feature thoroughly on multiple devices
- Ensure smooth animations and responsive motion detection
- Prepare multiple photo sets for variety
- Test different lighting conditions

### During Production
- Record multiple takes for each scene
- Capture natural, unforced hand movements
- Ensure phone screen is clearly visible
- Maintain consistent app state (same photos, same position)

### Post-Production
- Smooth transitions between scenes
- Add subtle motion blur for phone tilting shots
- Sync sound effects with swipe actions
- Add text overlays with smooth animations
- Color grade to match app's aesthetic
- Add app branding elements (logo, colors)
