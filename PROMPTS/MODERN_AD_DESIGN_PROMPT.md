## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Contrast**: Ensure text is always readable over backgrounds
2. **Motion**: Keep animations subtle and professional
3. **Branding**: App icon should always be visible
4. **CTA**: Make the download button impossible to miss
5. **Testing**: Preview on actual phone screens before publishing
6. **Accessibility**: Ensure color-blind friendly (don't rely only on color)
