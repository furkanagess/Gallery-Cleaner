# Gallery Cleaner - App Features

Gallery Cleaner is a powerful, AI-driven mobile application built with modern Flutter technology that helps you organize, clean, and optimize your photo gallery with ease. The app features an intuitive Tinder-style swipe interface where you can quickly review hundreds of photos by swiping right to keep or left to delete, with smooth animations and haptic feedback optimized for one-handed operation. The AI-powered blur detection uses advanced Laplacian variance algorithms to automatically identify and remove blurry, low-quality photos with customizable sensitivity settings, batch processing capabilities, and visual indicators for safe review before deletion. The duplicate detection system employs multiple algorithms including dHash, pHash, aHash, and histogram comparison to find not just exact duplicates but also very similar photos, organizing them into groups and showing exactly how much storage space you'll save. The app provides comprehensive results and statistics with real-time tracking of photos processed, storage analytics, scan history, and visual progress charts. All deletions are protected with confirmation dialogs, batch deletion support, undo options, and full integration with iOS "Recently Deleted" and Android trash features, while all processing happens 100% locally on your device with no cloud uploads for complete privacy. Premium features unlock unlimited deletions and scans without daily limits or waiting periods, an ad-free experience, and lifetime access with a one-time payment that includes all future updates. The app features a beautiful Material Design 3 interface with dark mode support, smooth animations, full accessibility support, and multi-language support for English, Turkish, and Spanish. Performance is optimized with efficient algorithms that process thousands of photos quickly, low memory usage through optimized thumbnails and caching, background processing capabilities, and isolate processing for heavy computations. Built with Flutter for cross-platform native performance, the app uses BLoC pattern for efficient state management, direct photo library access through Photo Manager integration, custom image processing algorithms, Firebase integration for crash reporting, RevenueCat for seamless premium purchases, and optional rewarded ads for free users. Whether you need to free up storage space by removing duplicates and blurry photos, organize years of memories with the swipe interface, improve gallery quality by keeping only the best photos, prepare for backup to save time and costs, or perform regular maintenance to keep your gallery optimized, Gallery Cleaner provides a fast, smart, safe, private, beautiful, effective, and affordable solution with no account required and minimal permissions, making it the ideal choice for transforming your photo gallery management experience.
