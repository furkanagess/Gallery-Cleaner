# App Store New Year Event - Gallery Cleaner

## Event Name
**Start Fresh: New Year Gallery Cleanup**

## Short Description
Begin the new year with a clean, organized photo gallery. Remove duplicates, blurry photos, and free up space for new memories.

## Long Description

**Welcome the New Year with a Clean Gallery!**

Start 2025 on the right foot by giving your photo gallery a fresh start. Gallery Cleaner helps you organize, clean, and optimize your photo library in time for the new year.

**Why Start Fresh?**
- **Make Room for New Memories**: Delete old blurry photos and duplicates to free up valuable storage space
- **Organize Your Moments**: Clean up your gallery so you can easily find and enjoy your favorite photos
- **Better Performance**: A streamlined gallery means faster app performance and smoother browsing

**What You Can Do:**
✨ **Quick Swipe Review**: Easily swipe through photos to keep or delete - it's fast and intuitive
📸 **Remove Duplicates**: Automatically find and remove duplicate photos
🎯 **Delete Blurry Photos**: Get rid of blurry, out-of-focus images automatically
📁 **Organize Albums**: Keep your memories neatly organized in albums

**Perfect for New Year:**
- Clear out old photos you no longer need
- Make space for capturing new memories
- Start the year with a clean, organized gallery
- Free up storage for the photos you'll take in 2025

**Special New Year Features:**
- Daily delete limits reset for a fresh start
- Premium features to unlock unlimited cleaning power
- Beautiful, modern interface for an enjoyable cleaning experience

Transform your cluttered gallery into a clean, organized collection. Download Gallery Cleaner now and start the new year with a fresh, optimized photo library!
