## New Year 2026 - Gallery Cleaner

Create an ultra-modern, festive in-app event banner for "Gallery Cleaner" mobile app's Swipe Tab, positioned above the PhotoSwipeDeck widget. The banner celebrates New Year 2026 with a special "1000 Photos Delete Event" promotion, featuring Christmas elements, snow theme, and swipe-to-delete functionality showcase.

**Format & Dimensions:**

- Aspect ratio: Optimized for mobile portrait banner (approximately 16:3 or 5:1 ratio)
- Width: Full screen width (mobile portrait)
- Height: Approximately 120-160px (compact, non-intrusive)
- High resolution, crisp details, professional in-app quality
- Dark theme optimized with primary blue color scheme (#5D9CEC)
- Designed to sit above PhotoSwipeDeck without obstructing swipe functionality

**Main Composition:**
A modern, festive horizontal banner celebrating New Year 2026 with strong New Year theme elements (countdown clock, fireworks, confetti, party balloons, "Happy New Year" text, midnight celebration atmosphere) combined with Christmas elements (subtle Christmas tree, Santa hat, snowflakes) and snow theme, seamlessly integrated with Gallery Cleaner's swipe-to-delete feature. The design should feel contemporary, premium, and celebration-focused with prominent New Year 2026 celebration while showcasing the "1000 Photos Delete Event" promotion.
