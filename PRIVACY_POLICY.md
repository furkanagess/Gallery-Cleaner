# Gallery Cleaner Privacy Policy

_Last updated: November 11, 2025_

## 1. Introduction
Gallery Cleaner ("App") helps you review, organise, and clean your media library by detecting duplicate and blurry photos.  Protecting your privacy is one of our core principles.  This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes, how it is used, and what choices you have.

## 2. Data We Do Not Collect
- **No account required:** The App does not create user accounts or require any sign-up.
- **No cloud processing:** All photo analysis runs locally on your device.  We do not upload your media to any server.
- **No behavioural profiling:** We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

## 3. Data We Access Locally
To provide its core functionality, the App needs limited access to:

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Photo Library (read-only) | Detect blurry or duplicate images, display thumbnails | Processed entirely on-device. No copies are exported. |
| Deletion Requests | Remove items you selected for deletion | The system Photos/Media APIs handle the deletion. We never keep a copy. |
| App Preferences | Store settings such as premium status, theme, language, scan limits | Encrypted secure storage (Keychain/Keystore) or SharedPreferences on the device. |

## 4. Permissions
- **Photos/Media Library:** Required to analyse and manage your gallery. You can revoke this permission at any time from system settings. Without it, the App cannot function as intended.
- **Network (optional):** Used only for verifying premium purchases and (if enabled) loading rewarded advertisements. No personal content is transmitted.

## 5. Premium Purchases
When you buy the lifetime premium upgrade, your purchase is processed by the respective app store (Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not receive your payment details. We only store a boolean flag that indicates whether premium is active.

## 6. Advertising (if enabled)
The App may show optional rewarded ads to increase daily scan limits. Ad networks may collect anonymised usage data such as device identifiers or approximate location. You can choose not to watch ads and rely on the basic quota.

## 7. Analytics
The App currently does **not** integrate third-party analytics SDKs. If we add anonymous analytics in the future, this document will be updated and you will be asked for consent where required.

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion
- All photo analysis results are kept in volatile memory and cleared when you leave the App or revoke permission.
- Local preferences remain on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data.
- If you uninstall the App, all locally stored preferences and cache are removed automatically by the operating system.

## 9. Children’s Privacy
The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 10. Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Revoke gallery permissions at any time in system settings.
- Delete the App to remove all locally stored preferences.
- Opt out of rewarded ads by not initiating them.

## 11. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product or legal changes. Updates will be posted in this repository and in the App settings screen, along with the “Last updated” date above.
