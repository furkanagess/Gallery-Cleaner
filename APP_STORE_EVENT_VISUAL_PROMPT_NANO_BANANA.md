# App Store Event Visual Prompt - Nano Banana
## New Year 2026 - Gallery Cleaner

Create an ultra-modern, festive App Store event visual for "Gallery Cleaner" mobile app featuring New Year 2026 celebration with Christmas elements, snow theme, and app features showcase.

**Format & Dimensions:**
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 landscape (2048x1152px) for App Store featured event
- Alternative: 9:16 portrait (1152x2048px) for mobile banners
- High resolution, crisp details, professional App Store quality
- Dark theme optimized with primary blue color scheme

**Main Composition:**
A modern, festive scene combining New Year 2026 celebration with Christmas elements (Christmas tree, Santa hat, Santa Claus) and snow theme, seamlessly integrated with Gallery Cleaner app features (swipe to delete, blur removal, duplicate detection). The design should feel contemporary, premium, and celebration-focused while showcasing app functionality.

**Visual Elements:**

1. **New Year 2026 Celebration:**
   - Prominent "2026" numerals in modern, elegant typography (can be integrated into design or as watermark)
   - "Happy New Year" text in stylish, contemporary font
   - Countdown or celebration elements suggesting fresh start
   - Festive atmosphere with celebration vibes

2. **Christmas Elements (Modern Integration):**
   - **Christmas Tree**: Modern, stylized Christmas tree in background or corner
     * Contemporary design with geometric shapes or minimalist style
     * Decorated with app-themed ornaments (photo icons, app logo)
     * Subtle glow or lights (using app's blue color #5D9CEC)
     * Not overwhelming - integrated elegantly
   
   - **Santa Hat**: 
     * Worn by a character or floating as decorative element
     * Modern, stylized design matching app aesthetic
     * Can be on a photo card or as icon
     * Red and white, but styled to match modern design
   
   - **Santa Claus**:
     * Modern, friendly Santa character (can be minimalist illustration)
     * Holding a phone or interacting with photo gallery
     * Contemporary art style - not cartoonish
     * Can be showing app features (swipe gesture, organizing photos)
     * Positioned to complement, not dominate the design

3. **Snow Theme:**
   - Gentle snowflakes falling in background (subtle, not overwhelming)
   - Snow-covered elements or winter atmosphere
   - Frosted glass effects on UI elements (glassmorphism)
   - Cool, winter color accents (white, light blue, silver)
   - Snow particles or winter sparkle effects
   - Icy, crisp feeling suggesting freshness and cleanliness

4. **App Features Showcase (Modern & Clear):**

   **A. Swipe to Delete Feature:**
   - Photo card in center showing swipe gesture
   - Left swipe: Red "X" icon with delete indicator and motion trail
   - Right swipe: Green checkmark with keep indicator and motion trail
   - Modern swipe cards stacked in Tinder-style deck
   - Motion blur effects showing swipe action
   - Clean, minimalist gesture indicators
   - Floating action buttons or swipe hints

   **B. Remove Blur Feature:**
   - Blurry photo with blur detection icon overlay
   - Before/After comparison: blurry photo transitioning to sharp
   - Blur indicator badge or warning icon
   - Visual representation of blur detection (scan lines, detection waves)
   - Modern UI showing blur removal process
   - Clean, organized result after blur removal

   **C. Duplicate Detection Feature:**
   - Two overlapping photos with "Duplicate" badge or "x2" indicator
   - Visual connection lines showing duplicate detection
   - One photo being removed/faded out
   - Duplicate icon or symbol (modern, minimalist)
   - Clean gallery after duplicates removed
   - Storage space indicator showing space freed

     * Primary to Accent: #5D9CEC to #A5F1E9
